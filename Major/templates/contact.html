About

Welcome to Coding Grading, a revolutionary platform for coders to showcase their skills and experience in one place. We understand that in today's fast-paced and competitive world, coders need a platform that helps them stand out from the crowd. That's why we built Coding Grading - a website that collects data from different coding platforms to grade coders based on their performance across various skills, including coding, DSA, and competitive coding. Our platform offers the best user interface and premium problems for free to overcome some of the drawbacks present in other coding platforms. With Coding Grading, coders can keep track of their overall performance in a mannered and graphical way for better understanding.

Our main objective is to benefit both coders and companies. For coders, we provide an easy and efficient way to showcase their skills to potential employers. By making an account on Coding Grading, coders can keep track of their overall performance and track their progress over time. For companies, we offer a comprehensive platform that allows them to check a candidate's skill set from a single platform, saving valuable time in the hiring process. With Coding Grading, companies can easily filter and shortlist candidates based on their skill level, making the hiring process more efficient and effective. So whether you're a coder looking to showcase your skills or a company looking for top talent, Coding Grading has got you covered. Join our platform today and take the first step towards achieving your goals!
